Michie Lee

Hi there! I’m Michie Lee and I am a data-driven creative with a unique and unconventional background who is dedicated to helping improve people’s lives.

My UX Skills

User Empathy

My background in psychology and work with disabled children has allowed me to hone my active listening skills and provided me with unique opportunities to put myself in other people’s shoes to see the world from different perspectives. Thanks to this experience I am able to empathize with users of diverse abilities and backgrounds.

User Research

I spent six years in a world where every decision I made had to be backed up by data. As a result, I can craft user interview questions and surveys that get to the heart of what our users need while providing us with the necessary data. I am also very experienced in writing usability testing plans that target measurable, observable behaviours with clear pass/fail parameters.

Implementing Feedback

“Designers plan, and Users laugh”...that’s the saying, right? Either way...

Recognizing, interpreting, and successfully acting on feedback is an integral part of the design process as it fuels iterations and often leads to better products. I am experienced in quickly and effectively implementing feedback in a way that increases program success.
